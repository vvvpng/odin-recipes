Description

Learn how to make a simple and delicious omelette.

Ingredients

  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Bacon
  • Minced Garlic
  • Salted Butter
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Prepare the fillings: Cook the bacon, garlic, and onion over medium heat until the bacon is cooked and onion is soft. Set aside.
  2. Cook the omelette: Begin by whisking the eggs homogenous. Add salt and pepper. Then melt the butter in a skillet and add the eggs in the pan.
  3. Cover the pan and cook medium-low heat: Cook the omelette and use a spatula to push the edges.
  4. Let omelette rest and sprinkle cheese on it: Let the omelette rest for 5 minutes and put cheese on top.
