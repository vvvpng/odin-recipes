Description

This is a recipe on how to make perfect lasagna! You'll learn how to make an easy, homemade lasagna in just a few steps.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Parlsey
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water!
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain and add the pasta sauce & let it simmer.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna nodoles and bake until browned and bubbly.
